⚡ Credit Stacking — The Big 2026 Move

You can stack multiple credits on a single hotel booking this year. If the hotel qualifies for both The Edit AND the Chase Travel Select Brands credit:

  • $250 — The Edit credit (one of your two)
  • $250 — Chase Travel Hotels credit (IHG/Montage/Pendry/Omni/etc.)
  • $300 — Annual travel credit (if not yet used)
  • = $800 in statement credits on one 2-night stay

Montage Healdsburg (Napa/Sonoma, ~1.5 hours from Orinda) qualifies for both The Edit AND Montage brand credit. That's a $800 stack on one weekend trip. Book prepaid through Chase Travel.

How to book The Edit correctly

Go to chase.com/travel → search hotels → filter or look for "The Edit" badge. Must book as prepaid (non-refundable or refundable prepaid). Minimum 2-night stay. The $250 credit posts as a statement credit within 1–2 billing cycles. You still earn UR points on the full charge amount before the credit.

$300 StubHub Credit — How to Use

Setup (one time, do this now)

  • Open Chase app → Benefits → find StubHub credit → activate
  • After activation, any StubHub or viagogo purchase charged to your CSR automatically gets the credit
  • $150 for Jan–Jun purchases, $150 for Jul–Dec — credits don't roll over if unused
  • Concert tickets, Warriors/Giants/49ers games, theater, comedy shows — all count
  • Use by June 30 for first $150, then again by December 31 for second $150
